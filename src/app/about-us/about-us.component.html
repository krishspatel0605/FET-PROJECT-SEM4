What We Offer

Our Fitness Data Tracking application offers an easy-to-use platform that provides automated updates on fitness data. We provide a standardised battery of tests that can be easily completed by students, coaches, and sports administrators. The data recorded is compiled and presented in the form of a 'Fitness Index' that allows for comparisons across regions, districts, ages, and genders.

Benefits

  • Centralised repository for fitness data across the state
  • Identification of potential talent
  • Regular check-ins on healthy growth and development
  • Identification of early signs of health issues
  • Potential solutions to augment fitness levels
